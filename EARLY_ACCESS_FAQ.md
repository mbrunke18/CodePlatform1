## Program Overview

### What is the VEXOR Early Access Program?

The Early Access Program is a 90-day validation partnership with 10 Fortune 1000 companies to pilot the VEXOR Executive Decision Operations Platform before general availability. Partners receive full platform access, dedicated implementation support, and early adopter pricing while helping shape the product roadmap.

### Why should we participate?

**Strategic Advantages:**
- **First-mover advantage**: Build strategic execution capabilities before competitors
- **Category leadership**: Help define the "Executive Decision Operations" category
- **Risk-free validation**: 90 days to prove ROI with full support
- **Early adopter pricing**: Locked-in pricing below future market rates
- **Product influence**: Direct input on features and roadmap priorities

**Operational Benefits:**
- Complete platform access (all 7 ecosystem components)
- Dedicated implementation team
- Custom playbook development support
- Priority feature requests
- Executive briefing services

### Is there a cost to participate?

Early Access Partners receive **significantly discounted pricing** (40-60% below projected general availability rates) locked in for 12 months post-validation. The 90-day validation period includes full implementation support at no additional cost.

## Technical Requirements

### What systems does VEXOR integrate with?

**Current Connectors (Pre-Built):**
- Slack (messaging & notifications)
- Microsoft Teams (collaboration)
- Jira (task orchestration)
- Salesforce (CRM data)
- HubSpot (marketing automation)
- Google Calendar (meeting coordination)
- Outlook/Exchange (email & calendar)
- ServiceNow (IT service management)
- Workday (HR systems)

**Custom Integrations:**
- REST API webhooks
- Custom enterprise systems
- Legacy platforms (via API)

### What are the security/compliance requirements?

VEXOR meets enterprise security standards:
- **Data Encryption:** AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit
- **Authentication:** SSO via OIDC (supports Azure AD, Okta, Google Workspace)
- **Compliance:** SOC 2 Type II (in progress), GDPR-compliant
- **Access Control:** Role-based permissions (RBAC)
- **Audit Logging:** Complete activity trails for compliance
- **Data Residency:** Cloud-hosted (configurable regions)

### Can we use VEXOR with on-premise systems?

Yes. VEXOR supports hybrid deployment with secure connectors to on-premise systems via:
- VPN tunnels
- API gateways
- Secure webhook endpoints

**Protection Scenarios:**
- Competitor threat response

- Cybersecurity incident response

**Transformation Scenarios:**

### How many playbooks should we start with?

We recommend starting with **3-5 high-priority scenarios** during the validation period:
- 1-2 **Protection** scenarios (crisis/threat response)
- 1-2 **Growth** scenarios (opportunity capture)
- 1 **Transformation** scenario (strategic change)

### Can we customize scenarios for our industry?

Absolutely. VEXOR includes 13 pre-built strategic scenarios as templates, but the What-If Analyzer enables unlimited custom scenario development. During implementation, we work with your team to develop industry-specific playbooks tailored to your strategic priorities.

## Risk & Commitment

### What if it doesn't work for us?

The 90-day validation is designed to be **risk-free**:
- No long-term commitment required
- Exit option at end of validation period
- Data export assistance
- No penalties for non-renewal

### What data/IP commitments are required?

- **Your data stays yours:** VEXOR does not claim ownership of customer data
- **No case study requirement:** Participation in marketing case studies is optional
- **Confidentiality:** All strategic scenarios and data remain confidential
- **Data portability:** Full export capabilities at any time

### Can we keep Early Access pricing after validation?

Yes. Early Access Partners lock in discounted pricing for **12 months minimum** after successful validation, with option to extend at similarly favorable rates based on partnership performance.
