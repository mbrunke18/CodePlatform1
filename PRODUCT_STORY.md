**Bastion = The First Executive Decision Operations Platform**

Creating a new market category like Salesforce defined "CRM" and ServiceNow defined "ITSM," Bastion defines "Executive Decision Operations"—the complete platform infrastructure for Fortune 1000 strategic execution.

  - Pre-assigned roles (47 stakeholders mapped)

  - Success metrics (KPIs and measurement)
- Practice mode: Run drills, test scenarios, build muscle memory
- Executive Preparedness Score™: Competitive 0-100 readiness metric

  - Financial indicators (budget risks, market volatility)
  - Operational metrics (supply chain, production, quality)
  - Regulatory landscape (compliance, investigations, policy changes)
- Executive-defined triggers: "Alert me when competitor AI capabilities exceed threshold X"
- Pattern recognition: AI connects dots humans miss
- Proactive alerts: Warning before crisis, not after

**Phase 3: Execute (Crisis Activation)**
- Trigger fires → Playbook auto-suggested → One-click activation
- 12 minutes to full coordination:

  - Minute 3-5: War room assembled (virtual or physical)
  - Minute 6-8: Budget approved, resources mobilized
  - Minute 9-12: Response live, media briefed, customers informed
- Real-time task tracking with countdown timers
