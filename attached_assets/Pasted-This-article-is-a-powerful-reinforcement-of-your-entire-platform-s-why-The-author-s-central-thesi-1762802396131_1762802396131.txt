2. Autonomous AI Tasks within Playbooks
The article's examples (City of Kyle, Reddit) are not just about AI suggesting things; they are about AI autonomously resolving tasks. Your executionPlanTasks are currently assigned to human roles.
• Enhancement: Leverage your isAutomated and automationConfig fields in executionPlanTasks to assign tasks directly to VEXOR AI Agents.
• How it Works:
1. A task in the Playbook Library like "Draft initial internal crisis memo" is no longer assigned to the requiredRoleId "CHRO".
2. It is assigned to the "VEXOR AI Comms Agent" with isAutomated: true.
3. When the playbook activates, your enterpriseJobService routes this task to your openAIService, which uses the playbookCommunicationTemplates to generate a draft.
4. The AI agent marks its task "Complete" in seconds.
5. The next task in the executionPlanTasks sequence, "T+4:00 - Review and Approve AI-drafted comms," is then assigned to the human CHRO.
• Business Value: This is a perfect realization of the article's point about redefining jobs. You are automating the "mundane" (drafting) and leaving the "human judgment" (reviewing and approving) to the executive. This makes your 12-minute execution timeline even more credible, as AI-driven tasks are completed instantly.
