# PRODUCTION NOTES

## Visual Style

- Cinematic color grade (clean, modern, high contrast)
- Mix of premium stock footage and motion graphics
- Typography: Bold, modern sans-serif
- Product UI shown as stylized motion graphics, not literal screenshots
- Energy: Confident and forward-moving, not fear-based

## Music Direction

- Modern orchestral hybrid OR driving electronic
- Build from anticipation → momentum → resolution
- Avoid dark/ominous - this is about winning, not just surviving

## Voiceover Direction

- Authoritative but warm
- Confident, not aggressive
- Measured pace with purposeful pauses
- Could be Marty’s voice for authenticity

## Key Visual Moments

**OFFENSE imagery:**

- Expanding into new markets (globe, maps)
- M&A announcements, deal closings
- Product launches
- Competitive wins

**DEFENSE imagery:**

- Rapid response coordination
- Threat neutralization
- Protection, shields
- Crisis resolved

**SPECIAL TEAMS imagery:**

- Digital transformation visuals
- AI/innovation imagery
- Game-changing moments
- Breakthrough achievements

## Stock Footage Sources

- Artgrid.io (cinematic quality)
- Storyblocks.com (volume)
- Pexels.com (free)

# TRADE SHOW / CONFERENCE USAGE

## Booth Setup

- Primary screen: Loop 90-second film (no audio, captions essential)
- Secondary screens: Rotate 30-second versions
- Demo station: Live product walkthrough

## Talking Points

**When someone watches:**
“You’re seeing the challenge every organization faces - opportunities and threats move fast, but execution takes forever. We compress 72 hours into 12 minutes.”

**Transition to demo:**
“Let me show you what happens when a playbook activates - whether you’re going on offense to seize an opportunity, playing defense against a threat, or driving a transformation.”

## Conference Session Usage

- Open with 90-second or 2-minute film
- Close with 30-second spot
- Reinforces message before and after your presentation

# MESSAGING SUMMARY

## Core Positioning

M Platform is the Strategic Execution Operating System - enabling organizations to execute strategic initiatives 360x faster across offense (seizing opportunities), defense (responding to threats), and special teams (driving transformation).

## Key Messages

**Primary:** “The speed to execute.”

**Supporting:**

- 72 hours → 12 minutes
- 148 playbooks across offense, defense, and special teams
- Pre-mapped stakeholders, pre-staged decisions, coordinated execution
- First movers don’t wait for meetings - they execute

## Tone

- Confident, not fearful
- About winning, not just surviving
- Proactive, not reactive
- Speed as competitive advantage

## Avoid

- Crisis-only framing
- Fear-based messaging
- Reactive/defensive-only positioning
- “When things go wrong” as the only hook
