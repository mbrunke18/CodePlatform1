## CRITICAL GAPS TO ADDRESS

### 1. THE AI DETECTION STORY NEEDS PROOF

**Current claim:** “AI pattern recognition across 10,000+ scenarios”

**Buyer question you can’t answer yet:** “Show me the AI working. What signals did it detect last week? How accurate is it?”

**Reality check:** You’re selling AI monitoring without a functioning detection engine. This is the fastest path to credibility death in enterprise sales.

**Recommendation:**

- **Reframe Detection as “Human-AI Partnership”** (keep this language)
- Emphasize that playbooks are AI-informed but human-triggered during pilot phase
- Position full AI detection as Phase 2 roadmap item
- Lead with “assisted intelligence” not “artificial intelligence”

**Revised Detection Language:**

> “D - DETECT (‘Scout the Field’)  
> Human insight amplified by AI-powered pattern matching. We help you spot what matters—and ignore what doesn’t.  
> • Scenario pattern library (10,000+ historical triggers)  
> • Competitive intelligence aggregation  
> • Early warning signal dashboards  
> • Human-triggered playbook activation”

This is honest, still valuable, and doesn’t promise tech you haven’t built.

### 2. THE $75K PILOT IS UNDERPRICED FOR THE CLAIM

**Your value claim:** $270M–$900M execution gap closed  
**Your pilot price:** $75K for 90 days  
**Math problem:** If you deliver even 5% improvement on $270M ($13.5M), you’re charging 0.5% of value delivered.

**Enterprise buyer psychology:**

- $75K pilot signals “small vendor trying something new”
- $250K pilot signals “serious platform with proven ROI”
- $500K pilot signals “McKinsey-class partner”

**Recommendation:**
Given you have no case studies yet, structure tiered pilot pricing:

**Tier 1: Proof of Concept ($125K, 90 days)**

- Single domain (choose Offense OR Defense)
- 20 playbooks staged
- One live execution
- Success metric: <24hr response time (halfway to 12min goal)

**Tier 2: Domain Pilot ($250K, 90 days)**

- Full domain deployment (all Offense/Defense/Special Teams playbooks)
- Three live executions
- Success metric: 12-minute response achieved once

**Tier 3: Enterprise Pilot ($500K, 180 days)**

- All 166 playbooks across 9 domains
- Integration with existing systems (ServiceNow, Slack, etc.)
- Dedicated CSM + quarterly business reviews
- Success metric: Consistent <12min response across 5+ scenarios

Start conversations at $250K. Drop to $125K if needed. Never lead with $75K—it undercuts your category positioning.
