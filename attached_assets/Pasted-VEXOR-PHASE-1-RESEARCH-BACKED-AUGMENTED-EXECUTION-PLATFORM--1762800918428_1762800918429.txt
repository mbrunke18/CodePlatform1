STRATEGIC CONTEXT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THREE RESEARCH FOUNDATIONS:

1. McKinsey "Organize to Value" (June 2025)
   - Finding: 30% strategy-to-execution gap in high-performing companies
   - Framework: 12 elements drive organizational performance
   - Key outcomes: Clarity, Speed, Skills, Commitment
   - Link: https://www.mckinsey.com

2. Torsten D. "Augmented Execution: Human + Machine" (Oct 2025)
   - Philosophy: "Augmentation not automation"
   - Key insight: "Where should humans stay in the loop?"
   - Framework: Trust through transparency, human-in-the-loop governance
   - Focus: Psychological safety, shared accountability

3. MIT Tech Review "The Great Acceleration: CIO Perspectives on Gen AI" (2025)
   - Data: 600+ CIOs from Fortune 1000
   - Findings: 22% efficiency gains, 17% innovation velocity increase
   - Key: 14.3% ROI improvement with strong governance
   - Evidence: 64% scaling AI enterprise-wide by 2026

POSITIONING SHIFT:
From: "VEXOR automates crisis coordination"
To: "VEXOR augments organizational execution through human-in-the-loop 
     coordination, delivering McKinsey's four outcomes and MIT's validated 
     CIO performance metrics"
