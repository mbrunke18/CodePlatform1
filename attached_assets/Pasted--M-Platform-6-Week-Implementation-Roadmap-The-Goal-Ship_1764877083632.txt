|---------|--------------|

| Weak Signal Visualization | Needs actual signal sources |

| Competitive benchmarking | Requires customer data you don't have |

| Slack/Teams notifications | Can add quickly once core works |
| Role-based views | One good view > many partial views |

## Final Note

This roadmap gets you from "promising prototype" to "fundable product demo" in 6 weeks. It's aggressive but achievable.

The key insight: **You don't need more features. You need fewer features that work completely.**

The 4-phase lifecycle IS your product. Everything else is either proof it works (Jira integration) or packaging (mobile, demo scenarios).

Ship the loop. Then raise.
