## The Problem

### The 72-Hour Gap

When significant strategic events occurâ€”competitor acquisitions, regulatory changes, cybersecurity incidents, executive departuresâ€”most organizations experience a predictable pattern:

| Hour | Typical Enterprise Response |
|------|----------------------------|
| 0-4 | Discovery and initial confusion |
| 4-12 | Ad-hoc calls to identify who should be involved |
| 12-24 | Scramble to find relevant documents and data |
| 24-48 | Debate about authority, budget, and approach |
| 48-72 | Finally begin coordinated response |

**The cost of this gap:**
- Competitors gain 3-day head start on response
- Market opportunities close before action begins
- Reputational damage compounds with each hour of silence
- Regulatory exposure increases without documented response
- Employee anxiety spreads without clear communication

### Why This Happens

The problem isn't lack of talent or technology. It's that strategic response requires coordination across multiple dimensions simultaneously:

1. **People** â€” Who has authority? Who needs to be informed? Who executes?
2. **Information** â€” What templates exist? What data is needed? What's the situation?
3. **Resources** â€” What budget is available? Which vendors are on standby? What systems are needed?
4. **Decisions** â€” What's pre-approved? What needs escalation? What are the decision criteria?

Traditional tools (project management, communication platforms, BI dashboards) address individual dimensions. None orchestrate across all four simultaneously under time pressure.

### Phase 1: PREPARE

**Purpose:** Ensure everything needed for rapid response is staged and ready before events occur.

**Components:**
- **Stakeholder Matrix** â€” Every playbook has pre-assigned owners, alternates, and RACI for all roles
- **Document Templates** â€” Press releases, board memos, customer communications pre-drafted
- **Resource Staging** â€” Budget pre-approved, vendors on standby contracts, systems access verified
- **Authority Delegation** â€” Spending limits, decision rights, escalation thresholds defined

**Readiness Scoring:** M calculates real-time readiness scores for each playbook based on PREPARE phase completion. Organizations know their strategic readiness posture at a glance.

### Phase 2: MONITOR

**Purpose:** Continuously watch for signals that indicate playbook activation may be needed.

**Components:**
- **Signal Types** â€” Competitive, regulatory, market, operational, financial, security, reputation, talent, supply chain, technology
- **Data Sources** â€” News feeds, market data, internal systems, social monitoring, regulatory filings
- **Trigger Conditions** â€” Threshold-based, pattern-based, event-based, manual, scheduled
- **Alert Routing** â€” Role-based notifications with confirmation requirements and auto-escalation

**Intelligence Layer:** M monitors 92+ data points across 16 categories, detecting patterns that indicate emerging strategic events before they become crises.

### Phase 3: EXECUTE

**Purpose:** Orchestrate coordinated organizational response with pre-staged resources.

**Execute Sub-Phases:**

| Sub-Phase | Timing | Focus |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| **Immediate** | 0-15 min | Critical first actions, key stakeholder notification |
| **Coordinate** | 15 min - 2 hrs | Cross-functional alignment, resource mobilization |
| **Resolve** | 2-24 hrs | Core response activities, external communications |
| **Close** | 24-72 hrs | Wrap-up, documentation, transition to normal operations |

**Integration Capability:** Upon activation, M auto-creates projects in enterprise tools (Jira, Asana, ServiceNow) with pre-assigned tasks, owners, and deadlines. No manual project setup required.

### Phase 4: LEARN

**Purpose:** Capture execution learnings and continuously improve playbooks.

**Components:**
- **Structured Debriefs** â€” Pre-configured questions for post-event analysis
- **Metrics Capture** â€” Automated tracking of response times, completion rates, outcomes
- **Playbook Updates** â€” Learnings feed directly into playbook improvements
- **Pattern Recognition** â€” Historical execution data improves future trigger calibration
